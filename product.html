Adjustable Props

We are offering a wide array of Scaffolding Props. Steel-Caff Props are manufactured from top quality, continuously welded steels tubes.Sturdy 150 x 150 x 6 mm Head & plates welded on to inner(40nb) members having matching holes for fastening of props on top of each other to gain additional heights. A36 mm center hole is provided to insert stirrup head fitment or U head to hold wooden or steel bearer of slab centering. Nail holes are provided on Head and Foot plates for nailing wooden runners/bearers on to props. Quick and large adjustment in height can be made by inserting lock into holes provided in the inner member and fine adjustments are made by ratting the malleable iron nut on the outer member.
